RECYCLING, TOGETHER

The global emission of methane gases from textile landfills have been increasing incrementally.
Every effort counts in reducing pollution. Therefore, the participation of each individual is vital.
LUCE helps individuals make smarter choices in disposing preloved clothes.

OUR MECHANISM

We help users engage in the effort of recycling preloved clothes by providing initiative in the form of
coupons, rebates and vouchers. Users can join in on the action by heeding the following steps:

1) SORT

Users sort their preloved clothes into three main categories which are men, women and children. This helps in the weighing process used to determine the amount of points received.

2) SCAN

Users will be provided with a receipt which has a QR code by the operator on-duty after sending their clothes. An email prompt will appear when the user scans the QR code. The user then has to send the verification code from the receipt through the email prompt.

3) REWARD

After the code has been certified, the server administrator will upload the coupon reward that corresponds with the user's donation. The coupon rewards are given based on a weight-point system.
